Hey how familiar are you with Aresty? If you haven't found it yet, the Summer Science Research Program is a summer program for first years only! It is competitve, but you get paid a $3000 stipend. You work 9-5 each day and live in the Livi C apartments (across from the Livi Student Center). It's STEM topics only, and it's a 1:1 student to professor ratio, so you'll be paired with only one professor to oversee you. Because of this personal nature, the interviews are in person. Applications open up around January, and don't be scared to apply! They may list a GPA, but it's only recommended, not required, so still apply even if you don't meet it! There are three info sessions this fall.

The Learning Centers are quite useful. They have free academic tutoring for lots of classes. For example, CS majors have to take calc, and the LCs offer drop-in tutoring for classes like those. You could be getting an A, C, or F in the class and still be allowed in. You can also sign up for academic coaching sessions to help manage everything going on in your hectic schedules right now! These are not drop-in, but are also completely free. There's also general writing tutors outside of the writing centers. Last but not least, there's the LA program, which is hidden somewhere on this page *eyes emoji*. There's a learning center on each campus, and they're open 8am-9pm during the week.
